About

From mechanical engineering to enterprise software ownership.

I started in mechanical engineering — project management, technical drawings, and facility design — where I learned to break ambiguous problems into precise, buildable specifications. After completing the Dev10 software engineering program, I moved into software full-time and joined Prudential Financial's Group Insurance technology organization in 2022.

I joined as an Associate Software Engineer and was promoted to Senior Software Engineer in 2024, working across enterprise Salesforce, Java/Spring Boot services, React applications, and AWS deployment architecture. In 2025 I began leading the enterprise Salesforce platform team, and for roughly the last year and a half I've carried acting Tech Lead-level responsibilities — contributing to design decisions, reviewing pull requests, mentoring and onboarding junior engineers, owning production releases, and translating business needs into reliable technical plans.

That ownership has been recognized with Prudential's highest performance rating, "Exceptional Impact," for three consecutive years (2023–2025) across both business and leadership outcomes. I care about shipping maintainable systems, raising code quality through reviews and testing, being a dependable owner for the people and platforms I support, and keeping production stable under enterprise reliability standards.
